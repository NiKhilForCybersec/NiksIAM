Privileged Access Management (PAM)

PAM solutions protect high-value accounts and systems through privileged credential vaulting, session management, and just-in-time access.

Core PAM Capabilities

CapabilityDescriptionSecurity Benefit
Credential VaultingCentralized encrypted storage for privileged credentialsEliminates credential sprawl
Password RotationAutomatic rotation of privileged passwordsLimits credential exposure window
Session RecordingVideo and keystroke recording of privileged sessionsForensics and compliance
Just-In-Time AccessGrant privileged access only when neededReduces standing privileges
Privileged ElevationElevate permissions without exposing passwordsLeast privilege enforcement
