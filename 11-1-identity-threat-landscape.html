Threat Monitoring:

  • Review Identity Protection alerts (risky users, risky sign-ins)
  • Investigate impossible travel and anonymous IP alerts
  • Monitor for credential leak notifications
  • Track threat intelligence on new identity attacks

Incident Response:

  • Investigate suspected account compromise
  • Revoke sessions, reset credentials, audit changes
  • Trace attack timeline through audit logs
  • Identify scope of compromise

Proactive Defense:

  • Tune detection rules to reduce false positives
  • Implement new controls based on threat intel
  • Run phishing simulations
  • Update Conditional Access policies
