ğŸ‘¨â€ğŸ’» What You Actually Do

Daily Tasks:

  • Process access request queue (approve/deny/clarify)
  • Handle escalations: "My new hire can't log in"
  • Troubleshoot failed provisioning operations
  • Verify urgent terminations are complete

Common Scenarios:

  • "New hire starting Monday": Verify HR record, check workflow triggered, confirm access ready
  • "Employee transferring departments": Remove old access, add new access, verify no conflicts
  • "Contractor extension": Extend access expiry, get re-approval if required
  • "Urgent termination": Immediately disable, coordinate with HR/Legal, document timeline

Weekly Reviews:

  • Pending access requests > 48 hours (escalate)
  • Failed provisioning jobs
  • Upcoming contractor expirations
  • Orphaned accounts (no manager, no department)

Metrics You Track:

  • Time-to-access for new hires
  • Time-to-revoke for terminations
  • Provisioning success rate
  • Access request backlog
