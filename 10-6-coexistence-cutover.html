Rollback Procedure

âš ï¸ Rollback Requirements
  • Keep old IdP running: Don't decommission until confident (30+ days)
  • DNS TTL: Lower TTL before cutover for fast DNS changes
  • App configuration: Document how to revert each app's IdP config
  • Decision criteria: Define what triggers rollback (X% failure rate)
  • Time limit: Decide rollback within first 4 hours if critical issues
