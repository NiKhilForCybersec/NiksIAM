Role Engineering:

  • Analyze access patterns for new role opportunities
  • Meet with business stakeholders to define roles
  • Document role definitions and ownership
  • Configure roles in IGA platform

Role Maintenance:

  • Review roles during app onboarding (what roles need this app?)
  • Update roles when permissions change
  • Retire obsolete roles
  • Handle role exception requests

Role Analytics:

  • Monitor role usage (how many assigned?)
  • Identify role sprawl (too many similar roles)
  • Track birthright vs requested role ratios
  • Measure time-to-access improvements

Role Design Best Practices

ğŸ“ Design Principles
  • Job-based: Roles should reflect job functions, not individuals
  • Least privilege: Include minimum access needed for the role
  • No overlaps: Avoid having multiple roles with same access
  • Clear ownership: Every role needs a business owner
  • Documented: Role purpose, members, and included access
  • Reviewed: Annual role certification by owner

Interview Tips

ğŸ’¡ Key Interview Points
  • Role mining: Discover roles from existing access patterns
  • Role engineering: Design roles based on job functions
  • Birthright: Automatic roles based on attributes (no request needed)
  • Role explosion: Problem when too many roles created
  • Business vs IT roles: Business = job function, IT = technical entitlements
