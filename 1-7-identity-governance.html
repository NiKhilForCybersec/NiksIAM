Identity Governance & Administration (IGA)

ğŸ¯ What This Page Covers

Identity Governance ensures the right people have the right access for the right reasons - and can prove it. This covers access certifications, entitlement management, compliance reporting, and how IGA fits into enterprise security.

What is Identity Governance?

Identity Governance and Administration (IGA) is the practice of managing digital identities and their access rights across the enterprise. It answers the critical question: "Who has access to what, and should they?"

Core IGA Capabilities

CapabilityWhat It DoesBusiness Value
Access Certifications Periodic review of user access by managers/owners Compliance (SOX, HIPAA), removes excess access
Access Requests Self-service portal for requesting access with approvals Faster access, audit trail, reduced tickets
Lifecycle Management Automated provisioning/deprovisioning based on HR events Day-1 productivity, instant termination
Role Management Group access into business roles for easier assignment Simpler administration, consistent access
Separation of Duties Prevent toxic access combinations Fraud prevention, compliance
Compliance Reporting Generate audit reports showing who has what access Pass audits, prove compliance

Access Certifications Deep Dive

Access certifications (also called access reviews or attestations) are the cornerstone of governance. Managers and application owners periodically review and confirm that users still need their access.
