Key Vault Features

FeatureDescriptionBenefit
Encrypted StorageAES-256 encryption at restCredentials protected if vault compromised
Auto RotationChange passwords on schedule or after useLimits exposure window
Check-out/Check-inOne user at a time, with time limitsAccountability, prevents sharing

Audit TrailLog all access to credentialsCompliance, forensics
