Interview Tips

ğŸ’¡ How to Nail PIM Questions

If asked "What is PIM?":

"Privileged Identity Management is Microsoft's Just-In-Time access solution. Instead of giving admins permanent privileged access, users are made 'eligible' for roles. When they need access, they activate the role - which requires MFA, a justification, and potentially approval. The access is time-limited and automatically expires. This reduces the attack surface because privileged accounts aren't privileged 24/7."

If asked "Eligible vs Active?":

"Eligible means you CAN request access - like having a ticket to a show. Active means you currently HAVE access - like being in your seat. Users move from eligible to active through the activation process, which enforces controls like MFA and approval. Active access automatically expires back to just being eligible."

If asked "Why use PIM?":

"Three main reasons: Security - reduces standing privileged access and attack surface. Compliance - provides full audit trail of who had access when and why. Least privilege - access is just-in-time and just-enough, not permanent."
